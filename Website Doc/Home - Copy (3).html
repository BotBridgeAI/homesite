About Us

At BotBridge.net, we provide a platform for AI chatbots supported by the community. Our system integrates third-party databases for character cards, allowing users to engage in creative conversations and build their own unique chat experiences.

Features

  • Community-supported AI chatbots

  • Free expression and creativity without restrictions
  • Create and share your own databases

Content Disclaimer

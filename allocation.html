How Your Donations Are Used

We are committed to transparency regarding how donations are utilized. Here's a breakdown of how your contributions are allocated:

Supporting Third-Party Databases (70-90%)

The majority of your donations go directly into supporting and enhancing third-party databases that drive our platform. This includes:

  • Development and improvement of character cards
  • Maintenance and upgrades of database systems
  • Funding for new features and functionalities

Maintaining Servers (10-30%)

A portion of your donations is used to cover the costs of maintaining the servers that power our site and provide additional official AI servers. This includes:

  • Hosting and server maintenance costs
  • Upgrades to ensure high performance and reliability
  • Providing free access to official AI servers for the community

Your contributions help us keep BotBridge.net running smoothly and support a vibrant community of AI chatbot enthusiasts. Thank you for your support!
